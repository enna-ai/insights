How to get started

1. Go to your Instagram account settings, access Account Center, and scroll down to choose Your Information and Permissions.

2. Click Download Your Information, then click Request a Download. Select Complete Copy and edit the Date Range to All time and finally, submit your request.

3. Once your data is ready (typically within 5 minutes), download the file, unzip it, and open the folder.

4. Find and open the Connections folder, followed uploading the followers_and_following folder. Upload the specified files below.
